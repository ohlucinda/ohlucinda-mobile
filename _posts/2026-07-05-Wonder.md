Interesting things always happen when I write lyrics.

I often say that my lyrics get there before I do.

I write whatever is closest to the surface at that moment.

I’m not trying to dig deeper while I’m writing.

Days, or sometimes months later, everything connects into a single thread.

On the other hand, there are things I’ve always wanted to say.

They’re the very things I can never put into words.

Then, when the time is right, the words come.

They arrive in words I never would have chosen myself.

There’s something mysterious about that.

It doesn’t say much.

Yet somehow, it gives clear language to something I know with complete certainty.

I call it interpretation.
